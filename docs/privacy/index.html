Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect when you use digitalhawk.ai and how we use it. If you have questions, email support@digitalhawk.ai.

Information we collect

  • • Scan form details: sending domain, your work email, chosen platform, and any bounce/error text you provide.
  • • Basic analytics: landing page path, referrer, and ad parameters (gclid, utm_*), if present in the URL.

How we use your information

  • • To diagnose email rejection issues and send you a fix plan.
  • • To measure which ads/keywords generate leads (to reduce wasted spend).
  • • To communicate about an optional paid Rescue Sprint if you request it.

Data retention

We retain form submissions as long as needed for troubleshooting and business records. If you want your submission removed, contact us.

Cookies and ad tracking

We may use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure ad performance. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may need to provide consent for certain cookies/tracking technologies.

Third parties

We may use third-party providers to collect form submissions and measure advertising performance (for example: form handling services and Google Ads). We share only what’s necessary to operate the service.
